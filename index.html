About

Our fine dining restaurant cater to exquisite palettes, whether it is the colours of our menu items or the colours of our staff uniforms. It is not just attire that sets our restaurants apart from others; the dishes served in them are carefully selected, often with a focus on international flavors.


With dishes like turbot cooked in red wine, filet mignon cooked over charcoal, and rack of lamb served with figs, there is certainly something for everyone at our establishments. While each cuisine has its own unique flavors and aesthetics, choosing a fine dining restaurant can provide you with unforgettable culinary experiences. So if you're looking for a special meal that will standalone from all the others, consider trying out one of our exclusive eateries.
